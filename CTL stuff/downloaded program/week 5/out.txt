 1 King Arthur: I am your king.
 2 Woman: Well, I didn't vote for you.
 3 King Arthur: You don't vote for kings.
 4 Woman: Well how'd you become king then?
 5 [Angelic music plays... ]
 6 King Arthur: The Lady of the Lake, her arm clad in the purest
 7 shimmering samite held aloft Excalibur from the bosom of the water,
 8 signifying by divine providence that I, Arthur, was to carry Excalibur.
 9 THAT is why I am your king.
10 Dennis: [interrupting] Listen, strange women lyin' in ponds distributin'
11 swords is no basis for a system of government. Supreme executive power
12 derives from a mandate from the masses, not from some farcical aquatic
13 ceremony.
